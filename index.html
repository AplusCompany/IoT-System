Aim & Scope

Sea-level rise is a critical issue for countries worldwide, especially Egypt. Thus, we worked on a project to accurately measure the changes in the Mediterranean Sea level and take studied actions. On this website, we intend to:
Awareness

Increase the awareness of people about the dangers of sea-level rise on our lives.

Communication

Share the results and data we collected from testing our project model.

Suggest Actions

Encourage people and the government to take measures against sea-level rise.
